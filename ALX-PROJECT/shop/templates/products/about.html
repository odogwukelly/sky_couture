Introduction

SkyCouture is a comprehensive e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for high-end fashion
enthusiasts. Our project aims to connect users with a curated selection of premium fashion products, offering a user-friendly
interface, secure payment processing, and personalized recommendations.

Key Features:

User Registration:

Users can create accounts by providing a username, email, password,
date of birth, and gender. Validation ensures unique usernames and emails,
Passwords are securely hashed before storage

User Login:

Registered users can log in with their email and password. Authentication is
handled securely, and users are remembered if they choose to do so.

Password Reset:

Users who forget their passwords can request a password reset via email. A time-limited
token is generated for password reset links. Security measures are implemented to prevent
misuse of reset tokens. These features collectively form the foundation of your e-commerce
platform, allowing users to register, log in, and recover their accounts when necessary. Your
project has come together to create a secure and user-friendly online fashion store, and it
showcases your team's dedication to web development and delivering value to users.

View product details

Users can view product details before adding to cart to either choose a preferred color
or select the number of products to be added to the cart.

Add to cart/Delete from cart

Users can add a product to the cart and also have the ability to delete a product from the cart.

Clear cart

Users can clear all products added to the cart at once.
